XJTaik

AI-Powered EAP Discussion Room

01. Motivation & Research

The Why

We chose the EAP student support track because our initial research revealed a critical pain point: most students experience high anxiety and "brain blanks" during English discussions.

The Gap Analysis

Strengths: AI transcription, feedback, stable platforms.

Limitations: No real-time intervention, no progress control.

Stakeholders

Primary: EAP Students.

Secondary: EAP Teachers.

02. User Requirements

3 Must-Have Playful Requirements

  • Low-pressure, judgment-free practice environment
  • User-controlled, gentle AI hints during silence
  • Supportive, clear feedback after discussion

03. Ideation & Alternatives

Crazy Eights

Design Alternatives

1. AI-driven real-time hint system
2. Peer feedback mechanism
3. Gamified participation points
4. Visual discussion progress tracker

Final Choice

We selected Alternative 1 (AI-driven real-time hint system) as the core function, combined with elements of Alternative 4.
